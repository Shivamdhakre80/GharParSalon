Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a salon service at home?

To book a salon service at home, simply visit our website or download our mobile app. Select the service you need, choose a date and time, provide your contact details, and confirm your booking. Our professional beauticians will then come to your home at the scheduled time.

What salon services do you offer at home?

We offer a wide range of salon services at home, including haircuts, hairstyling, hair coloring, facials, manicures, pedicures, waxing, threading, makeup, and more. You can explore our service menu for a complete list of offerings.

Are your beauticians qualified and experienced?

Yes, all our beauticians are highly qualified and experienced professionals. They undergo rigorous training and are skilled in providing salon services at home. Rest assured, you will receive top-quality service from our team.

Do I need to provide any equipment or products?

No, our beauticians will bring all the required equipment and products for the salon services. You don't need to worry about providing anything. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the salon experience in the comfort of your own home.

What safety measures do you follow during the pandemic?

Your safety is our top priority. Our beauticians strictly adhere to all the necessary safety protocols, including wearing masks, sanitizing equipment, and maintaining proper hygiene. We follow government guidelines to ensure a safe and hygienic salon experience for our customers.
